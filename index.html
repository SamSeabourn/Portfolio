Demo GB

After being inspired by the growing community of people developing games for the original Gameboy, I thought it would be great to build a platform for people to share and play demos that other developers had made. In addition I wanted to be able to produce physical copies of these games for the 1989 hardware, so I bought the hardware to do so.

This app demonstrates a Gameboy emulator running in the browser and is uploading, mounting and playing .gb files from cloud storage. This app was built in just over a week using Node.js, Express, Mongo.DB, Bcrypt and Cloudinary. When designing started with mobile first as I wanted to focus on making the app user friendly for those mobile users.

In addition to this I also build and published a game along side this project as a proof of concept. The game is a little story about myself and is fully playable on the site.
